COMPUTER CHOICE
- get the computer's choice by randomly choosing between 
    - rock
    - paper
    -scissors

PLAYER CHOICE
- player is prompted to enter their choice
- input is case INSENSITIVE

GAME
- Best of 5 rounds
- Winner is decided on who has the most wins by the end

LOGIC
1. Prompt the player for input
2. Generate computer input
3. Compare player and computer to see who wins
4. Give point to which player won
5. Repeat until game has been played 5 times
6. Compare scores to find winner
